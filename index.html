Welcome to Tabetomo!

What is "My Tabetomo?"

"My Tabetomo!" is a web app that analyses the food you eat to help you maintain a healthier lifestyle!
Create a virtual pet, called a "tabetomo", that you'll take care of through the foods you eat! Try to feed your tabetomo (and yourself) a healthy well-balanced diet!


Developed by: Liam Persad, Soraha Ebara and Nathaniel Davis

This project was developed as a Final Project for "CIS-3308 - Web Application Programming" class.
